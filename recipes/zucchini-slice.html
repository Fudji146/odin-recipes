ZUCCHINI SLICE and how it is prepared

Ingredients

  • Coles Eggs Free Range
  • 150g (1 cup) Coles White Self Raising Flour, sifted
  • 375g zucchini, grated
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 200g rindless bacon, chopped
  • 1 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 60ml (1/4 cup) vegetable oil

PREPARATION PROCESS

  1. Step 1 Preheat oven to 170C.
  2. Step 2 Beat the eggs in a large bowl until combined. Add the flour and beat until smooth, then add zucchini, onion, bacon, cheese and oil and stir to combine.
  3. Step 3 Grease and line a 30 x 20cm lamington pan. Pour into the prepared pan and bake in oven for 30 minutes or until cooked through.
